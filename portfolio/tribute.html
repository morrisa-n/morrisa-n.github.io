Thoughts

One of the FreeCodeCamp assignments is to build a tribute page for something. So I built a tribute to someone I've been missing for a while. His name was Wally, he was thirteen, and he was an incredible dog who was taken far too soon.

Anyone who says a pet is "just a pet" is wrong. He still holds a huge place in my heart, and I couldn't not pay tribute to my beloved "Doot Doot".

Sometimes you just have to let your heart do the driving.
