Thoughts

I was intrigued by the idea of a blog made from Jekyll and GitHub pages. With the assistance of this tutorial, I set up a basic blog for myself, then set out to tailor it more to my personality and my needs as a blogger. I wanted a layout that combined my love of bright colors, nature, and bold, chunky fonts. Once I found the photograph of the rainbow mug on Pixabay, I knew I had found the base for my layout.

The site layout is built entirely from CSS. I also used FontAwesome's icons, and a dash of Bootstrap here and there.
